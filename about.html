Introduction

Måneskin is an Italian rock band known for their energetic performances, edgy style, and genre-blending music. They achieved international fame after winning the Eurovision Song Contest in 2021 with their song "Zitti e Buoni". Måneskin's sound combines elements of rock, glam, punk, and pop, appealing to a wide range of listeners and marking them as one of the most popular rock bands in recent years.

History

Formation 2016

Damiano, Victoria, and Thomas first met each other in high school in Rome, with Ethan, who is from Frosinone, joining them later on. Even though they met in 2015, the band officially formed in 2016.

X Factor and Il ballo della vita 2017-2018

Måneskin rose to prominence after coming in second in the eleventh season of the Italian version of X Factor in 2017. The album, titled Il ballo della vita, and the single "Torna a casa" topped the charts in Italy.

First Europe tour 2019

During their first tour, held between 2018 and 2019, they sold-out over 70 dates along with selling more than 140,000 tickets.

Eurovision and global fame

Måneskin were designated as the Italian representatives at the Eurovision Song Contest 2021, held in Rotterdam, with "Zitti e buoni". On 15 May, Italy climbed to the top of the betting odds to win the contest.
